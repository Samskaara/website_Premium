🌿 Our Story

It all began with motherhood.

Though I was born and brought up in a culturally rich family, traditions were part of everyday life through festivals, shlokas, and values.

❓ The Questions That Changed Everything

  • “Why should we take a bath before breakfast?”
  • “Why do we chant shlokas?”
  • “Why do we follow rituals?”

I didn’t have clear answers at that time.

📚 The Journey of Rediscovery

I began researching our traditions, Sanatana Dharma, and the deeper meaning behind rituals.

🌍 COVID Made It More Relevant

  • We follow traditions but don’t fully understand them
  • We struggle to explain them to children

📱 Challenges Today

  • Screen exposure
  • Reduced attention span
  • Instant gratification

A space where children learn values, culture, and confidence.
